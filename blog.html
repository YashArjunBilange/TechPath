About TechPath

Empowering the next generation of AI engineers through comprehensive roadmaps, resources, and community

Our Mission

TechPath is dedicated to democratizing AI engineering education. We provide a comprehensive, year-by-year roadmap that guides students from their first year through to graduation and placement. Our platform combines curated learning resources, industry certifications, and hands-on projects to ensure students are job-ready.

What We Stand For

  • Quality Education: Only the best resources from top engineers and educators
  • Structured Learning: Clear, year-wise progression focused on practical skills
  • Industry Ready: Certifications and projects that matter to employers
  • Community First: Support and guidance from peers and mentors
